About K-Fold Cross Validation
What is K-Fold Cross Validation?

K-fold cross validation is an advanced technique that divides your dataset into K equal parts (folds) and trains K different models. Each model uses K-1 folds for training and the remaining fold for evaluation, rotating through all possible combinations.

Why use K-Fold Cross Validation?

This method produces more reliable performance metrics by evaluating your model on multiple data splits, reducing the risk of overfitting and providing confidence intervals for your metrics.
